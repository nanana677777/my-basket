Honestly, a bit of both: it's mostly here to keep my own wishes in one tidy place instead of scattered across apps. But if something on here catches someone's eye and they'd like to gift it through Throne, that's genuinely sweet too — no pressure either way, the list works just fine as a personal mood board.

👑

How Throne gifting works

What is Throne?

Throne is a wishlist and creator-gifting platform. It lets people add items from a wishlist (like this one) and send them as gifts, without needing to share personal shipping details directly.

How do I gift something from here?

Click on any item to open it, then hit "Add to Throne wishlist." That opens Throne's own gifting flow where you can complete the gift — I never see your payment or personal info, Throne handles all of that.

Is this list updated?

Yes — items get added, removed, or recategorized regularly as my own wishes change. There's no obligation tied to anything on it; it's just an evolving snapshot of what I currently love.
